Our services

What wedding planning services do you offer?

Full-service wedding planning, destination weddings (Goa, Kerala, Jaipur, Andaman), decor & stage design, floral installation, corporate events, private celebrations, and engagement setups. We can handle the wedding start-to-finish, or come in for a single track like decor.

How are you different from other wedding planners in Bangalore?

We are a husband-wife atelier — Poonam and Mayank, both there at every wedding. Our background is engineering, which means our run-of-show is built like a launch plan. Our heart is in the styling, which is why every wedding still feels hand-made.

Do you handle destination weddings?

Yes — destination weddings are about half of our work. Goa, Kerala, Jaipur, the Andamans most commonly; we have planned in twelve more cities on request.

Pricing & booking

How much do your wedding planning services cost?

It depends on guest count, location, and how much you want us to do. Our smallest engagements have started near ₹3 lakh; full destination weddings move into a different bracket. We share an honest, itemised estimate after the first call — no quote-on-the-spot.

What is your booking process?

A first call to understand your wedding, then a written proposal and an itemised estimate, then a signed agreement and a 30% retainer. After that, weekly working sessions until the wedding week.

What is your cancellation policy?

The retainer secures dates and vendors who block calendars for you, so it is non-refundable. Anything paid beyond the retainer is refundable on a sliding scale based on how close to the wedding the cancellation lands. Full terms in our agreement.

The planning process

When should we hire a wedding planner?

For a destination wedding, six to nine months out is comfortable. For a Bangalore wedding, four to six months. Sooner is always better — many of the best vendors are booked twelve months ahead.

Do you work with a specific list of vendors?

We have a curated network of photographers, caterers, florists, and venues we trust. We are happy to work with vendors you have already chosen, and we will always tell you honestly if a vendor we have not worked with concerns us.

How involved will we be in the planning process?

As involved as you want to be. Some couples want to approve every napkin colour; others want to disappear and trust us. Both are fine — we adapt.

Can you help us with a specific aspect, like only decor?

Yes. We offer single-track engagements for couples who already have a planner but need help with decor, florals, or a specific function (sangeet, mehendi, reception). It is a smaller engagement and we charge accordingly.
